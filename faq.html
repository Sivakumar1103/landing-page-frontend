Create, schedule, and publish your content for free of cost on our basic plan. To get started, go to Create new post, and then select an option.
  • Select your social accounts, and then create your post. In addition to text, you can include mentions or tags, links, images, videos, GIFs, emoji, and hashtags.
  • As you compose your post, you can preview how it will look on the right. When you're ready, you can publish it, save it as a draft, or schedule it for later.
  • View and manage your scheduled posts in a calendar
    • To view or manage your scheduled content, go to Publisher.
  • You can create new posts and organize, edit, or delete existing posts right from the calendar.
  • Use the Planner view to review and manage your posts in a weekly calendar view. Select a time block in the calendar to create new content to publish at that selected time.
  • Use the Content view to see your past scheduled posts, view and manage your future scheduled and draft posts, set up RSS feeds, and upload messages in bulk.
  • Use the Promote view to allocate advertising spend to Facebook or LinkedIn Page posts. For more information on promoting your content, see Create and promote a post.
